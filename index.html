Vienna is a space for digital artists to share

We welcome all artists who use digital technology in their practice to discuss:

    - Art in any form that have been inspiring you
    - Projects you are currently working on/completed
    - Your stories and truths

One of the most important skills for an artist in the age of information is taste. Your ability to filter and select meaning and value from the world.

It's not something that can be taught in a classroom.

It can only be developed through exposure to other's perspectives, ideas, and truths

Which we will aim to faciliate through honest and transparent conversation

The early stages of an art project for university involve complex stages of ideation and planning
